// the idea

You bring the vision.

You don't grind through the build — you set the direction, and the crew does the rest, reviewing every commit the moment it lands.

A correctness audit, a quality pass, an editorial sweep — the rigor never stops, and never slows you down.

// the crew

Four skills.
One loop.

flow// the loop
Drives build → review for every commit, every chunk, every turn. Calls the rest of the crew for you.
auditor// correctness
A correctness-only review of every diff — logic errors, edge cases, broken contracts, intent drift.
editor// the prose
Strips AI-isms, narration, and conversational residue out of the comments and docs.
specialist// the outside read
Brings in OpenAI Codex — a different model — to cross-check the work, or for fresh eyes on a stubborn bug.

// the gate

A second model checks the work.

Most review tools ask one model to grade its own homework. crew doesn't.

As the crew builds, the specialist brings in OpenAI Codex — a different model, different blind spots — to cross-check the work and catch what Claude missed. That's the bar every chunk has to clear.

What ships has been audited, simplified, edited, and cross-examined.

// vibe with your crew

Bring your crew.

One prerequisite. The specialist runs the real OpenAI Codex CLI. The bridge to it installs with crew, but the CLI is yours to provide — the codex command on your PATH, plus OpenAI auth. Without it, the cross-model pass skips and everything else still runs.
